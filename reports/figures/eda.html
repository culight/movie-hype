All of the continuous variables in the dataset have a weak correlation at best. The strongest correlation seems to involve general characteristics like the number of people who liked, voted on, or reviewed the movie and the financial success of the movie. More specific characteristics like the amount of attention paid to the actual cast (actor/director like) and duration had a weak negative correlation to the interest score. Oddly, the imdb score has almost no correlation with the amount of interest people had in the movies.
